Drake

Rapper / Singer

My real name is Aubrey Drake Grahman, alot of people call me Drake. I am an award winning Canadian rapper and singer from Ontario, Toronto.

My Strengths

Making albums is my calling card. With 8 albums that are currently on Apple Music, there are plenty of songs you can listen to. I'm also experienced and comfortable with being behind the camera. I've played a role in a total of 10 movies. One of them being "Anchorman 2: The Legend Continues".

Collaborations

  • In 2013, I signed a shoe deal with both Nike and Air Jordan, allowing me to advertise and wear both Nike and Jordan shoes.
  • In 2020, I collabed with "A Bathing Ape". The collaboration allowed me to make an OVO x BAPE clothing collection.
